I am a self-taught developer, working as a software engineer in Greece. I love creating stuff, solving complicated problems, and fixing every single bug I come across.
My programming journey began by taking online courses on platforms like edx, freeCodeCamp and coursera.

In 2019, after putting in a lot of hard work, countless hours building projects and searching on Google (we all know you do this too), I managed to fullfil my dream of getting a software engineering position. This role requires heavy usage of tools by cloud providers, such as AWS and GCP, and I have been loving learning more about the possibilities of cloud development. Lately, what interests me most is advancing my web development skills, and specifically exploring cloud services and tools to implement exciting projects and applications.
