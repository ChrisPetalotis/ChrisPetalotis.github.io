Get to know me...

I am a self-taught developer, with work experience as a cloud engineer. I love creating stuff, solving complicated problems, and fixing every single bug I come across.

Currently, I am living in Amsterdam, Netherlands, and pursuing my Master's degree on Computer Science, with a specialization on Software Engineering and Green IT. This is a joint program provided by the University of Amsterdam (UvA) and the Vrije University Amsterdam (VU).

My programming journey began by taking online courses on platforms such as edx, freeCodeCamp and coursera. I have explored topics ranging from web development (front-end and back-end) to mobile development to cloud engineering.

In 2019, after putting in a lot of hard work, countless hours building projects and searching on Google (we all know you do this too), I managed to fullfil my dream of getting a software engineering job. This role required heavy usage of tools by cloud providers, such as AWS and GCP, and I loved learning about the capabilities of cloud development.

Lately, what interests me most is advancing my web development skills. More specifically, I am exploring cloud services and tools to implement exciting projects and applications. Furthermore, I am learning about front-end concepts and ways to create responsive UIs. To this direction, I have started teaching myself how to use ReactJS.
